Evans' Favorite Movies of All Time

My top 3 movies of all time with reasons why!

1. Passion of Christ

Truly inspiring, and captures a feeling of realization for the reason we are redeemed.

2. Lucy

Although its concepts are unfounded, the color grading feels like a match made in heaven for the story.

3. Ao Ashi

An intersting perspective on the discipline and diligence it requires to be excellent even when blessed with a natural spark.
